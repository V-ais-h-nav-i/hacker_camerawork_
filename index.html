Portrait

Portrait photography is about capturing the essence, personality, identity and attitude of a person utilizing backgrounds, lighting and posing. A photo becomes a portrait when the subject along with the background, lighting and emotion can evoke a sense of connection between the viewer and the portrait photograph.

Wildlife

Animal photography is a type of photography that capture images of animals in their natural or domestic settings. It can include wildlife photography, which focuses on animals in the wild, or pet photography, which takes photos of people's pets. Animal photography can show the animals in action or in detail. Animal photography can also help us see animals in a new way and understand their behavior and characteristics.

Birds

Bird photography is a type of wildlife photography specifically associated with photographing birds. Bird photographers use cameras with fast shutter speeds, good low-light performance, and image resolution, as well as zoom lenses to photograph birds from a distance without affecting their behavior.

Architecture

Architectural photography is a genre of photography that focuses on capturing photographs of buildings or structures. It also involves photographing the interiors and exteriors of buildings, bridges, structures, and cityscapes. When compared to other photography fields, this kind of photography is less dynamic as it demands you to spend a lot of time contemplating a scene or an environment.

Sunrise And Sunset

Sunrise and Sunset provide more dramatic lighting for your images than any other time of day. The low angle of the sun in the sky refracts light in such a way that covers your subject in glorious rich color.

Street

street photography, a genre of photography that records everyday life in a public place. The very publicness of the setting enables the photographer to take candid pictures of strangers, often without their knowledge. Street photographers do not necessarily have a social purpose in mind, but they prefer to isolate and capture moments which might otherwise go unnoticed.

Butterfly

Butterfly is one of the most beautiful and wonderful creature in the world. The natural colors, patterns and shapes of Butterfly are so delicate and attractive that we never hesitate to photographing it. They are sharp fast and flit from one place to another place in search of nectar. All the day they search for nectar rich flower and plants. They are found abundance on a sunny day because there body needs the heat of the sun. On early cool morning they widely open the wings towards the sun in an exposed manner to warm their flight muscles. This is the best time to get close to it and photograph it creatively. In early morning, two things is in our favors.

Hi I'm Pavan

During the trying times of the Covid-19 pandemic, a period many of us found to be particularly challenging, I embarked on a quest to uncover my true passion. It was during these uncertain days that I stumbled upon the enchanting world of photography. In the early days, my fascination with photography manifested as a mere curiosity. I eagerly joined friends on their photoshoots, knowing little more about the camera than the names Canon and Nikon. My focus was not on the technical aspects; rather, I reveled in the art of capturing moments frozen in time. I vividly recall my third photoshoot, a pivotal moment in my journey. Guided by a friend, I took my first photograph. It was as though a spark had been ignited within me. The very next day, I sought out a camera for rent, determined to explore this newfound passion. I began taking photographs, experimenting with different modes, and eagerly examining the results. As I delved deeper into the realm of photography, my hunger for knowledge grew. I voraciously devoured resources on camera models, specifications, and techniques. Armed with Google and YouTube, I absorbed both theoretical concepts and practical know-how. Gradually, my skills improved with each snapshot, and I began to decipher the intricate checklist of elements necessary for crafting exceptional images. The path became clearer with every click of the shutter, and I knew I had unearthed my true passion. This revelation prompted me to invest in my own camera, solidifying my commitment to this captivating craft. Since then, my journey with photography has been a continuous and enriching exploration.
